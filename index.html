Croissant🥐

A croissant is a buttery, flaky French viennoiserie pastry known for its crescent shape and laminated yeast-leavened dough. Made from flour, yeast, sugar, milk, and high-quality butter, it features dozens of delicate layers achieved by rolling and folding. They are typically baked until deep golden brown, yielding a light, airy texture with a crisp exterior.
